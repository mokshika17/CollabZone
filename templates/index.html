Welcome to CollabZone

CollabZone is a platform that connects sponsors and influencers for creating impactful advertising campaigns. Here, sponsors can create and manage campaigns, search for influencers, and send ad requests. Influencers can receive ad requests, negotiate terms, and accept campaigns that align with their niche.

Features of CollabZone

  • Admin dashboard for monitoring users and campaigns
  • Sponsor management of campaigns and ad requests
  • Influencer profile management and ad request handling
  • Search functionality for finding relevant influencers and campaigns
